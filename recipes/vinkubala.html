Vinkubala (Mopane Worms)

Description

Mopane Worms popularly known as vinkubala in Zambia are an incredibly important source of protein. They can be eaten as a snack or served with Nshima (pap) as relish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of vinkubala (Mopane Worms)
    • 1 medium sized tomato (diced)
  • 1 medium sized onion (chopped)
  • Salt
  • 3 tablespoon cooking oil

Steps

  1. Wash the vinkubala twice in cold water
  2. Remove from cold water and soak in hot water for at least 5-7 minutes
  3. Add 3 tablespoons of cooking oil in a frying pan and heat it
  4. put the vinkubala in the pan and fry. You can add some salt
  5. Continue to fry until they become crunchy. This can take about 7-10 minutes depending on the heat
  6. Add some onion
  7. Continue to fry until the onions begin to brown
  8. Then add the tomatoes and fry until the tomatoes are cooked
  9. The vinkubala is now ready to serve. Enjoy!
